How to buy on Soko Yetu

A quick guide to finding what you need and buying with confidence.

Finding what you're looking for

🔍 Search or browse by category

Use the search bar for something specific, or browse by category from the homepage grid. You can filter results by price range, location, and sort order on the browse page.

🖼️ Check the full photo gallery

Tap through all the photos on a listing, not just the first one - sellers often include close-ups of any wear or extra angles that tell you more about the item's real condition.

💬 Message the seller with questions

Anything not covered in the description? Use the chat button on the listing to ask before you commit to meeting up - it saves both of you time.

Making an offer

If a listing is marked negotiable, you can send the seller an offer directly from the listing page instead of just asking over chat. Sellers can accept, decline, or counter your offer.

Meeting the seller

🤝 Agree on a safe, public place

Choose a busy, well-lit location convenient for both of you. Avoid isolated meeting spots, especially for higher-value items.

🔎 Inspect before you pay

Check the item matches the ad - condition, features, and any specs mentioned. For electronics, make sure it actually powers on and works. Never pay before you've seen the item in person.
