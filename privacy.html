Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Phase 1 note: Soko Yetu's account and data storage systems are still being built. Right now, no personal data you enter on the site is sent to or stored on a server — it stays only in your browser for the current session. This policy describes how things will work once the backend is live.

1. What we collect

Once accounts are fully active, we'll collect: your name, phone number, and optional email when you register; the content of ads you post, including photos and descriptions; and basic usage data (like pages visited) to help us improve the platform.

2. How we use your information

We use your information to operate the marketplace — creating your account, displaying your ads, connecting you with buyers or sellers, and keeping the platform safe. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. What other users can see

Your name and the contact details you choose to show are visible to other users on ads you post. You can control whether your phone number is shown publicly or only revealed when a buyer requests it, in your Settings.

4. Phone numbers and contact masking

By default, phone numbers on listings are masked until a buyer chooses to reveal them. This is designed to reduce automated scraping of contact details and give sellers more control over who contacts them.

5. Data storage and security

Once our backend is live, your data will be stored on secured servers with industry-standard protections. We'll never store passwords in plain text. You'll be able to request a copy of your data, or request that we delete your account and associated data, at any time.

6. Cookies and tracking

We use minimal technical storage (such as session storage) to keep the site working smoothly — for example, remembering items in a form you're filling out. We don't use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

7. Children's privacy

Soko Yetu is intended for users who are 18 or older. We don't knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Changes to this policy

As Soko Yetu's features grow — particularly around payments and messaging — this policy will be updated to reflect new data practices. We'll note the "last updated" date at the top whenever this happens.

9. Contact us about privacy

Questions about how your data is handled? Reach out via our Contact page.
