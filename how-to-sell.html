How to sell on Soko Yetu

A quick guide to posting ads that actually get responses.

Getting started

1. Tap "Sell"

Tap the red "SELL" button in the header, or "Post an ad" from your dashboard, to start the listing form. You'll pick a category, add details, upload photos, and set your location and price.

2. Wait for approval

Every new ad is reviewed by our team before it goes live, usually quickly. This keeps spam and prohibited items off the platform, and helps buyers trust what they see on Soko Yetu.

Writing an ad that gets responses

📸 Use clear, honest photos

Add several photos taken in good light, showing the item from different angles - including any flaws. Buyers are far more likely to message you when they can see exactly what they're getting.

📝 Be specific in your title and description

Include the brand, model, condition, and any key details a buyer would want to know. "iPhone 12 Pro, 128GB, screen protector since day one" gets more attention than just "Phone for sale."

💰 Price it realistically

Check similar listings in your category before setting a price. Ads priced far above similar items tend to sit unsold, while a fair price - especially if marked negotiable - attracts faster offers.

📍 Keep your location accurate

Buyers filter listings by city, so an accurate location helps the right people find your ad, and makes it easier to agree on a convenient place to meet.

Once your ad is live

Reply to messages promptly - buyers often message several sellers at once, and quick responses win more sales. You can edit your ad's price, description, or photos at any time from your dashboard, and mark it as sold once it's gone.
